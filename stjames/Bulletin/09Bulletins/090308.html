OPERATION RICE BOWL calls us to walk in solidarity with our brothers and sisters in need. During this second week of Lent, we focus on the Philippines. In the Philippines, your support of Operation Rice Bowl enables Catholic Relief Services to work with small farmers to improve their farming techniques, which has resulted in tenfold increases in production. CRS also works with small farmers on marketing their produce, and helping successful farmers teach others. Let us accept the challenge of Operation Rice Bowl: to pray that we overcome indifference to the needs of others; to fast in solidarity with those who are hungry by eating simply; to learn about the needs of the poor, and to give money saved by fasting and eating simply to Operation Rice Bowl.

JOURNEY OF THE ELECT

Lent is a particularly special time for the Elect who have been journeying towards baptism, Eucharist and confirmation at the Easter Vigil. As part of their preparation, the Church celebrates anointing with the oil of the Catechumen, blessings and exorcisms. Beginning next Sunday, we will join in prayer for our Elect as they participate in the ancient ceremonies called the Scrutinies that symbolize the spiritual purification taking place in their lives as they prepare for baptism. Through these Scrutinies, blessings and anointings, the Elect are encouraged to reflect more deeply on their own spiritual challenges and weaknesses. At the same time, these rites offer powerful prayers of faith, hope and strength on their behalf, calling upon God’s grace and mercy to penetrate their entire lives. Please continue to keep them in your prayers.
