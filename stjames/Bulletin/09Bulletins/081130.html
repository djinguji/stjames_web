VOLUNTEER CHORE SERVICE ORIENTATION. VCS is a program of Catholic Community Services and helps to connect volunteers with people who need chore services and companionship in the Seattle area. Volunteers help to ensure that elders and individuals with disabilities and/or long-term illnesses can remain in their homes among familiar surroundings rather than going to an institutionalized setting. In October, our VCS volunteers contributed over 125 hours of service. If you are interested in joining them in this rewarding ministry, please come to the Orientation to be held in the Pastoral Outreach Center on Tuesday, December 9 at 6:30 pm. (If you are in need of assistance through VCS, their intake number is: 1-888-649-6850). Information, Jennifer Ibach, 206-654-4640.

LISTENING DEVICES. Did you know the Cathedral has an assistive listening system? If you have difficulty hearing the spoken word, please talk to an usher about borrowing a listening device to use during Mass.

Each week during the coming Year of St. Paul, watch this space in the bulletin for reflection questions that will lead us deeper into the writings and example of St. Paul. You are also invited to visit the Place of Prayer in the west aisle and on the website.

  • St. Paul tells the Corinthians: “You are not lacking in any spiritual gift as you wait for the revelation of our Lord Jesus Christ.” Most often, we probably feel that we’re lacking quite a bit! What does Paul mean? What gifts have you received to prepare you for that “revelation”?
  • The Advent season is a period of preparation not only for Christmas, but also for the coming of the Lord at the end of time. What are you “doing” for Advent this year?

PARISH REMEMBRANCE: Throughout the year because we are the Cathedral Church, we remember in prayer at Mass and Vespers each of the parishes and missions of the Archdiocese of Seattle on a Sunday near their feast day. This week we remember in prayer the parishes of Immaculate Conception, Seattle and Everett; St. Nicholas, Gig Harbor; and Immaculate Conception Mission, Steilacoom.
