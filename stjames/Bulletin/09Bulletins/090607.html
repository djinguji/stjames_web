From the Pastor's Desk

June 7, 2009

Dear Friends,

It’s always good to have good news to share with you and today I have some very good news. Our parishioner, Todd Strange, will be ordained to the priesthood here in the Cathedral next Saturday, June 13.

Many of you know Todd. He came into the church back in 2001 through our RCIA program. After that he served as one of our Eucharistic Ministers and quietly reached out in loving service to people with special needs, including people in nursing homes and hospitals.

Todd is a gentle servant of the gospel and will be a wonderful priest.

Vocations to the priesthood are formed and fostered in many ways, but I firmly believe that a parish community has a critically important role to play in this regard. And Todd, I am sure, would be quick to affirm that about our parish, about you. You have had a part in leading him to the priesthood and his ordination is your celebration as well as his.

The ordination will take place in next Saturday at 10:00 in the morning. Seating will be limited because there are several men being ordained, but the next day, the feast of Corpus Christi, Todd will preside at a Mass of Thanksgiving at the noon Mass here in the Cathedral, and there will be plenty of seats for that! The Mass will be followed by a reception in the Cathedral Hall to which you are all invited. I hope you will be able to join Todd and his family next weekend as they and we give thanks to God for his vocation to service and leadership in the Church.


Father Michael G. Ryan
