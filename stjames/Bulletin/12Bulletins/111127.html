THE ST VINCENT DE PAUL conferences of St. James thank Cathedral parishioners for their generous donations last month. During October, we made 60 home visits to our low income neighbors. Your contributions allowed us to keep 44 individuals and families from being evicted from their homes. We prevented 13 utility shut offs and provided food to 20 households. In addition, we supplied 30 St. Vincent de Paul Thrift Store vouchers for beds, furniture, housewares and clothes. Members of the St. Vincent de Paul Society follow Jesus through service to those in need. Bearing witness to Christ’s compassionate and liberating love, we show our commitment through person-to-person contact, making our home visits in pairs. As calls for assistance continue to increase, we actively seek new members. Please consider joining this deeply rewarding ministry to the poor among us. Information, Rob Millar 206-954-8902 and Tammy Bendix 206-595-9158.

MENTAL HEALTH MINISTRY: PRAYER PARTNERS. Don’t be anxious about anything, but in everything, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God (Phil 4:6). We are a branch of the new Mental Health Ministry devoted to praying for those living with mental illness and emotional distress. We support the Mental Health Ministry by responding to special requests and needs that sometimes can only be answered with prayers. If you would like to request prayers for yourself, a loved one, or someone you care for go to Mental Health Ministry webpage and submit your prayer requests. All requests are strictly confidential.
