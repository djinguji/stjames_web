Getting to know Good Pope John

On September 30, thanks to some generous parishioners, a permanent shrine to Blessed John XXIII, the Pope who called the Second Vatican Council, will be established in the north aisle of the Cathedral.

Pope John confessed that he had some difficulty in falling asleep on the night of the memorable day that he announced the convocation of Vatican Council II. He said that he talked to himself in this way: “Giovanni, why don’t you sleep? Is it the Pope or the Holy Spirit who governs the Church? It’s the Holy Spirit, no? Well, then, go to sleep, Giovanni!”

From the Wit and Wisdom of Good Pope John
