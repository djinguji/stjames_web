WELCOME! If you are a visitor to the Cathedral, we want you to know how welcome you are — whether you have come from another part of the country, from across the world, or simply from another parish here in the Archdiocese. Thanks, in part, to the generosity of visitors like you, we are able to celebrate the Church's liturgy here in a fitting and beautiful way. We are also able to offer a wide range of services to needy people living in the central district of our city.

HELP A SENIOR IN NEED. Many in our community are in need of help with transportation, shopping, laundry, housework, and more. Age, illness, economics, and disabilities can all be barriers to accomplishing these routine, daily tasks. By volunteering just a few hours a month, you can make a huge difference in someone’s life, provide them peace of mind, and help them stay in their own home rather than going to an institutionalized setting. If you are interested in joining this rewarding ministry, please come to the orientation in the Mother Cabrini Room, Pastoral Outreach Center on Tuesday, December 1 at 6:30pm. Information Jennifer Ibach, 206-654-4640.
