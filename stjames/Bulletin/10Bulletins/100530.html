WELCOME! If you are a visitor to the Cathedral, we want you to know how welcome you are — whether you have come from another part of the country, from across the world, or simply from another parish here in the Archdiocese. Thanks, in part, to the generosity of visitors like you, we are able to celebrate the Church’s liturgy here in a fitting and beautiful way. We are also able to offer a wide range of services to needy people living in the central district of our city.

POTENTIAL VOLUNTEER TRIP TO HAITI. St. James is partnering with CRUDEM (Center for the Rural Development of Milot) to explore a volunteer trip to their Sacred Heart Hospital and Medical Center in northern Haiti. The group size is limited to 6 people and volunteers with physical therapy, nursing, primary care, and construction skills are needed most. We will be meeting on Tuesday, June 1 at 6:30pm in the Cathedral Place to gauge interest, discuss possibilities, and review basic trip information. Please RSVP if you plan to attend. Information, Jennifer Ibach 206-654-4640.

ST. JAMES CATHEDRAL YOUTH MUSIC PROGRAM CHOIR CAMP 2010. August 9-13, and Sunday morning, August 15, open to young singers grades 4-8, in the Archdiocese of Seattle. This week-long camp, led by some of the region’s finest Catholic music educators, offers excellent training in liturgical music. Students learn musical skills, note reading, singing techniques and participate in daily Morning Prayer in the Cathedral. For information and auditions call the Cathedral Music Office at 206-382-4874.
