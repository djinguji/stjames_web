CHARITY IN TRUTH. On July 17, Jim Thomas from the Archdiocese of Seattle will present a workshop at St. Bridget’s in Seattle on Pope Benedict’s first social encyclical, entitled Caritas in Veritate (Charity in Truth). The United States Conference of Catholic Bishops site says the encyclical “is a call to see the relationship between human and environmental ecologies and to link charity and truth in the pursuit of justice, the common good, and authentic human development. In doing so, the pope points out the responsibilities and limitations of government and the private market, challenges traditional ideologies of right and left, and calls all men and women to think and act anew.” The workshop will present an overview of the encyclical and discuss how it can be used to address the current economic crisis. Registration forms are due by July 6 and can be accessed online. Information, Patty Bowman 206-382-4515.
